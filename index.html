I received my bachelor's and master's degrees from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University, in 2007 and 2010. Now, I am a sixth year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Penn State University. I'm a member of MDL group in PSU. My advisor is Professor Yuan Xie, and my co-advisor is Professor Mary Jane Irwin.

My research interests focus on computer architecture with an emphasis on memory system design (both SRAM/DRAM-based and non-volatile memory-based) and energy efficiency in multi-core systems.
