I received my bachelor's degrees from School of Computer Science & Technology, Huazhong University of Science & Technology, in 2012 and my master's degrees from College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology, in 2014. Now, I am a first, second, third year Ph.D. student in Electronics and Information Systems Department, Ghent University. I'm a member of System Performance Lab in ELIS. My advisor is Professor Lieven Eeckhout.

My research interests focus on computer architecture with an emphasis on GPGPUs system design, including Network-on-Chip, Warp Scheduling and Cache Design. Currently, I am working on how to effectively support multitasking in GPUs.
