I received my bachelor's degrees from School of Computer Science & Technology, Huazhong University of Science & Technology, in 2012 and my master's degrees from College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology, in 2014. I finished my Ph.D. under the advisement of Professor Lieven Eeckhout. I am in China now and I am going to join the Chinese Academy of Military Science.

My research interests focus on computer architecture with an emphasis on GPGPUs system design, including Network-on-Chip, Warp Scheduling and Cache Design. Currently, I am working on how to effectively support multitasking in GPUs.
